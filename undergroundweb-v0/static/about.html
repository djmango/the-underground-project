We have lots of different channels. Here is a list of their purposes:

  1. Refer to the rules if in doubt
  2. #general is for general, non toxic conversation
  3. #spam is for talking about anything that does not violate the above rules.
  4. If there is no conversation going on, you may use #general as a place to post things relating to your voice chat
  5. #spam can also be used to move conversations if there is already one going on in #general
  6. #spam is for any links. Links anywhere other than #off-topic or #music-requests will be moved to #link-spam.
  7. #spam is for using bots to queue up music to play in the music listening voice channel and posting any links or images to accompany Voice Channel conversations.
  8. #memes is a shitposting group. Post all of your dank memes here!
  9. #politics is for political related discussion

We have lots of different rules. Here is the list of them:

  1. No spamming or flooding the chat with messages. Do not type in ALL CAPS
  2. No text walls or large paragraphs.
  3. Post in the correct channels.
  4. No bashing or heated arguments towards other people in the server.
  5. No racist or degrading content (racial terms are not allowed).
  6. Advertising other sites, discord servers etc. is only allowed with permission from staff.
  7. No begging or repeatedly asking for help.
  8. No offensive names.

Voice Chat Rules:

  1. No voice chat surfing or switching channels repeatedly.
  2. No annoying, loud or high pitch noises.
  3. Reduce the amount of background noise, if possible. Resort to push to talk in your settings to reduce the issue.
  4. You will be moved if the sound quality is poor to other members.

Bot Chat Rules:

  1. Do not spam commands.
  2. Do not use the bot to play loud music.
  3. Do not play music which time elapses over 30 minutes. Anything with the criteria of [Extended] [1 HOUR] [10 HOUR LOOP] is not allowed.

Warning:

  1. 3 Warnings and you're out THE OWNER OR ADMINISTRATORS RESERVE THE RIGHT TO AMEND THE RULES AT ANY TIME.
